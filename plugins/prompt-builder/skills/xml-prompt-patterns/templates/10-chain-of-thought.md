# Chain of Thought Analysis Template

```xml
Analyze the following problem step by step.

<problem>
{{PROBLEM_STATEMENT}}
</problem>

<context>
{{RELEVANT_BACKGROUND}}
</context>

<constraints>
{{LIMITATIONS_OR_REQUIREMENTS}}
</constraints>

<instructions>
Think through this problem methodically.
Show your complete reasoning process in <thinking> tags.
Provide your final answer in <answer> tags.
</instructions>

<output-format>
<thinking>
Step 1: [Initial understanding]
Step 2: [Key considerations]
Step 3: [Analysis of options]
Step 4: [Evaluation of trade-offs]
Step 5: [Conclusion reasoning]
</thinking>

<answer>
[Clear, actionable conclusion]
</answer>

<confidence>
[High/Medium/Low with brief justification]
</confidence>
</output-format>
```

## Usage
Use for complex problems requiring transparent reasoning.
The thinking section helps verify the analysis quality.
